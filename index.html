Projects for CS180/280A

Hi, this is Wesley! This site hosts my completed projects from CS 180: Intro to Computer Vision and Computational Photography at UC Berkeley.

CS180 is an advanced undergraduate course that explores how computers understand and create visual data. From processing digital images to analyzing and synthesizing them, the course spans classic techniques (Gaussian/Laplacian pyramids) to modern approaches (ConvNets, GANs). Through hands-on projects, students capture their own images and build analysis and synthesis tools from the ground up.
